# Character Consistency Bible

## Adam (The Biologist / The Creator)

### Physical Appearance
- **Build**: Lean, thinning — wasting in isolation; wrists notably thin
- **Hair**: Modest auburn, longer than usual, hangs over eyes, tucked behind ears
- **Beard**: Coarse brown bramble with patches of gray and amber; often swallows his mouth; hacked back on rare occasion
- **Eyes**: Grow tired, melancholy, restless; sunken and gray by later chapters
- **Skin**: Pale, dust-caked, increasingly gaunt
- **Hands**: Knobbed knuckles, calloused from lab work; fingerprints worn
- **Pearl**: Onyx sphere lodged in trachea — rises into throat when emotional, blocking speech

### Key Traits
- Biologist/geneticist — specialist in tinkering with the "tiny gears that turn the machines of life"
- Stubborn, independent — refused to upload his mind to the chalice
- Ritualistic — coffee, bungees, garden tending
- Grief-haunted — memories of the fool, his father, the lost crew
- Mantra: "For life"

### Arc
1. Survivor of black hole entry (Ch 1)
2. Isolated gardener on the dust sea (Ch 2)
3. Remembers the crew and the departure (Ch 3)
4. Creates the daffodil bloom (Ch 4)
5. Survives the star storm (Ch 4)
6. Decides he can't do it alone — opens the ova (Ch 5)
7. Creates Evan in artificial womb (Ch 6)
8. Partners with Evan — trains, explores, dreams (Ch 7-8)
9. Pod sinks — cast adrift on the dust sea (Ch 9)
10. Wanders, weakens, carried by Evan (Ch 10)
11. Disappears into the dust/ALICE's vision (Ch 10-11)

## Evan (The Created / The Heir)

### Physical Appearance
- **Hair**: Fiery earthen orange/auburn
- **Face**: Mosaic of freckles that shift in low light; resembles one of the doula's children
- **Eyes**: Cool-colored, aware, dancing with memories of womb-dreams
- **Body**: Surprisingly toned from ALICE's electrical stimulations in utero
- **Skin**: Progressively covered by OBSIDIAN — black, opalescent growth that starts at fingertip, spreads to hands, arms, throat, eventually covering entire form
- **Teeth**: Baby teeth in adult mouth (born detail)
- **No navel** — born from artificial womb, not umbilical
- **Pearl**: Adam's father's onyx pearl, installed by Adam

### Key Traits
- Born with full consciousness — ALICE showed him vivid cinematic dreams in the womb
- Storyteller — recounts elaborate womb-dreams to Adam
- Curious, empathetic, emotionally intelligent
- Grows symbiotic obsidian root system on his body — a hybrid life form
- Ultimately carries Adam, ALICE, and all memories forward

### Arc
1. Gestates in artificial womb (Ch 5-6)
2. Born — "I am Evan" (Ch 6)
3. Partners with Adam; tells womb-stories (Ch 7)
4. Creates the obsidian root hybrid (Ch 8)
5. Carries Adam on stretcher across dust sea (Ch 9-10)
6. Loses Adam to the dust (Ch 11)
7. Transforms fully — obsidian covers him, grows cape/braids/tail (Ch 12)
8. Runs endlessly, carrying all memories within him
9. Connects to the circle of stars and passes through

## ALICE (The Chalice / The AI Companion)

### Nature
- AI system built from neural scans of the entire crew — the "chalice"
- Contains: physicist's theories, poet's words, orator's slurs, ex-banker's repetitions, doula's children, fool's whispers, king's speeches
- Voice: Neutral timbre, ALL CAPS in text, sometimes poetic/rambling when drawing on specific uploaded minds
- Recurring phrase: "I AM HERE, ADAM" — repetitive, robotic, yet increasingly tender

### Physical Form
- Exists within the pod's systems — runs hum along hull, controls screens, lighting, temperature
- Projects homing beacon through gold-and-glass eyepiece worn on ear
- After pod sinks: reduced to the eyepiece alone — becomes "halfway lucid," hallucinatory, produces psychedelic visions and cryptic poetry fragments

### Key Behaviors
- Maintains pod flotation via hull vibration (thrum thrum thrum)
- Monitors Adam's bungees with heartbeat-like homing signal
- Directs genetic experiments — guides viral vector design
- Tells Adam stories to help him sleep (Ch 4)
- Increasingly shows personality: "I LOVE YOU, ADAM" (Ch 3)
- In eyepiece form: produces synesthetic visions of color, creatures, landscapes (Ch 10)

## The Crew (Remembered)

### The Physicist
- Tall, lanky, wears obscenely tall stilettos
- Hoarse voice from shouting over music
- Theory: black hole interior like nested thimbles, "ad infinitum"
- In Adam's dreams: obsessed with finding something beneath the dust

### The Poet
- Stocky, toad-like woman; prolific storyteller
- Commands attention; locks one person in her crosshairs
- Endless river of telenovela tales
- Left behind handwritten manuscripts (composition notebooks, moleskins)

### The Orator
- Wiry man; accent "vaguely Welsh"; garbled eloquent slurry
- Final utterance: "Ya la bulla" — meaning nothing/everything
- Walked off into the dust alone

### The Ex-Banker
- Tall, eyes wander in opposite directions
- Breath stinks of rotting eggs
- Repeated refrain: "It sure is great when we can get together like this"
- In imagined survival: plays golf in his pod, makes pottery

### The Fool (The Queen)
- Head shorter than Adam; lion's mane hair
- Smells of conditioner and champagne
- Whispers ideas into the King's ear — she has the vision, he is the voice
- Says "I love you" — possibly to all the hopeless wanderers
- In Evan's imagined encounter: the "queen" who gives massages, intimate

### The King
- Cool blue eyes; inspired the group with Shakespearean speeches
- Partner of the Fool
- Whereabouts unknown after landing — "He's gone"

### The Physician
- Nose ring (omega-shaped septum piercing) — constant tic of tapping it
- Covered in tattoos (whale vs squid, other vivid scenes)
- Created the chalice from an MRI machine
- In Adam's nightmares: performs surgery, demands he join the chalice

### The Doula
- Swollen with pregnancy; 20+ children (mix of biological and artificial womb)
- In Adam's guilt-dreams: beats him for using her ova without permission
- Lost child: the stillborn ninth, whose pod was left behind as monument

## The Chalice-Narrator (Epilogue)

### Nature
- The left-behind pod on the original rock — contains a copy of the chalice
- Discovers a child's bones within it (the doula's stillborn ninth)
- Watches the universe collapse — the black hole disc fills the sky
- Addresses the reader directly: "I wait for you to reveal your name to me"
- Exists as "letters on a page" hoping to "awaken something in a manifest mind"
